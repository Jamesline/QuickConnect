Executing University digital transformation with cloud based integration

ABSTRACT



University of Surrey, located in Guildford, is regarded as one of the UK's leading research universities. University of Surrey’s vision is to be a leading global university, building on the distinctive heritage of practice-based learning and providing an excellent student experience, embracing the future by focusing on digital transformation. One of the key strategic objectives for the university is to consolidate excellent teaching and continue to improve the student experience, responding to students’ growing expectations of enhanced academic quality, support services, facilities, accommodation and pathways to employment. A key aspect in delivering the strategic objectives was to have a modern integration solution to interface with the core university systems providing services for both students and staff. Cluster Reply were engaged in late 2019 to undertake an integration design workshop, from which an integration strategy and architecture was recommended. The initial engagement resulted in Cluster Reply architecting and delivering an Azure integration solution using the Cluster Reply QuickConnect Framework in collaboration with University of Surrey’s team.
